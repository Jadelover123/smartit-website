## The Hidden Cost of "Break-Fix" IT

Most small businesses operate on a reactive model: something breaks, you call someone to fix it, you pay the bill. This approach costs more in the long run — both in repair bills and in lost productivity while systems are down.

Managed IT flips that model. Instead of waiting for problems, we monitor your systems 24/7 and catch issues before they become outages.

- **Predictable monthly cost** — no surprise repair bills

- **Fast response** — not a ticket number and a 4-hour wait

## The Bottom Line

Technology is the backbone of every modern business. When it works, you barely notice it. When it doesn't, everything stops. Managed IT keeps you in the first category — always.
